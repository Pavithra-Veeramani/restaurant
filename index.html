Our Story


Welcome to Dosa hut which is a family owned business which was founded in 2010. We are located in the heart of the dublin city. We are dedicated to creating nine different varieties of crispy dosas. They are the crispiest dosas you'll ever experience, with an unforgettabe taste.

Our Food


Our first class Indian chefs use only the best ingredients. The best of ingredients specific to the region of Tamil nadu, famous for its spices,coconut milk and fresh curry leaves. Our chefs create each dish with pride, so you can savour traditional Indian flavours. We deliver hot, delicious Indian food right to your door step.
